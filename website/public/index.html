Welcome to Maths Ki Kaksha

We saw that most of the big online coaching institutes use elaborate setup to distance teacher from student. It is all done because the owners and teachers are two different people. Though this setup is nice for the owners it has its own serious demerits. For one, complex and heavy software makes teaching and writing, a tedious job and the student loses interest in the subject. No direct communication between the teacher and student / parents, long procedure for establishing such a communication and lastly the fear of consequences of being truthful about a child’s performance with a person eavesdropping in that communication are a few of the problems many Online Teachers shared with us.

That’s why we setup our Live CBSE Classes. If the Teacher is the Owner then All of the Above Problems Cease to Exist.
